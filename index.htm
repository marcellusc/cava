WHY GET A CAVAPOO FROM US ?

Cavoodle Ranch is a family owned & managed kennel. We Breed and Raise Mini Poodles, Cavalier King Charles Spaniels & show quality F1 Cavapoos ( Cavoodles as they are commonly called in Australia), to not just be the charm of every home but also an affectionate companion. Cavoodle Ranch is owned and managed by the Fournier Family. Yeah it’s a family business, but if you know our story & have worked with us you’ll know we treat our animals as our own children and it is far from being just a business. We have always had a connection with animals, I (Garry Fournier) was a professional thoroughbred racehorse trainer, while my wife worked at Boundary Road Vet Hospital. She also worked as a groom and exercise rider with at Wakefield racetrack. I am fully dedicated to breeding and raising our gorgeous Cavapoos on our 12 Acres Ranch. They are such an all around good natured breed, which makes them an excellent family pet. Poodles are known for their high intelligence and great non shedding coats while the cavaliers are known for their great temperament. We have been breeding cavapoos, for over 10 years now. Our dedication has earned us accolades and sweeps across local dog shows. 

HOW WE DO IT

We breed F1 Cavapoos  from pure bred parents. We prefer to keep it this way to make sure our cavapoos have a clean line and are more disease resistant.
All our dogs live in our home with us and are a part of our family. Our puppies are raised in our home , with lots of greendfield to run around from the time they are born until they go to their new loving families. It is important to us that they are well socialized with children, other animals, & common household noises, to make the transition to their new pet parents as easy as possible. We have a secured kennel outside for them to play. Puppies will be up to date on their vaccinations and de-worming. They will have their dew claws removed and a full check up from nose to tail. They will come with a one year health guarantee, sample of puppy food they are currently on, A manual specially made by us to raise a cavapooo and a lanket with the mom and siblings scent. You can contact us for more info, or view our current available puppies.

WHY GET A CAVAPOO PUPPY ?

Hypoallergenic :

Cavapoos are hypoallergenic and rarely shed and some do not shed at all, making them perfect for socialisation with allergic people. They also need far less grooming time.

Perfect Size : 

Their size makes them fit and adaptable for tight city apartments or for large country farms. You do not have to worry about moving on with them.

Temperament :

They are very friendly, playful and energetic. With other animals and especially kids. It’s the perfect family dog.

Life Span :

 They live longer than most dog breeds with a life span ranging from 13 to 16 years. They make real Forever friends and are loyal till the end.

Cute :

It’s no surprise everyone wish they had hair like them, its pleasing and therapeutic to cuddle and to be beside these beautiful creatures. Waste no time and get yours now.
